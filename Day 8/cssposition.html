CSS Position

  1. Static=>Default position, cannot use top, bottom, left, right properties
  2. Relative=> Relaive to its normal position, cannot affect other element
  3. Absolute=> Relative to nearest parent position
  4. Fixed=> Relative to the viewport
  5. Sticky=> when you scroll the page
